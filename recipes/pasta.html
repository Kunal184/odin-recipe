Classic Pasta Recipe

Home

Description

A simple and delicious pasta recipe with tomato sauce, garlic, and herbs. Perfect for a quick homemade Italian-style meal that’s both comforting and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of your choice
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions and drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Serve hot with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
